Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail coffee shop sales data to extract actionable insights that can improve business performance.
The dashboard helps in understanding customer behavior, sales trends, and product performance.

🎯 Objective

The main objective of this project is to:

Analyze sales trends across time (day & hour)
Identify peak sales periods
Compare performance across store locations
Understand customer spending behavior
Find best-selling products & categories
📊 Key Business Questions Solved
How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
What are the peak sales hours?
What is the total monthly revenue?
How do different store locations perform?
What is the average bill per person?
Which products generate the highest revenue?
How do sales vary across product categories?
📈 Dashboard Features
Interactive filters (Day-wise & Month-wise)
Hourly sales trend analysis
Top 5 products based on sales
Store-wise sales & footfall comparison
Category-wise distribution
Size-wise order distribution
🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Microsoft Excel
Data Cleaning
Pivot Tables
Charts & Visualizations
Dashboard Creation
📂 Dataset
Retail coffee shop sales dataset
Includes: Date, Time, Store Location, Product, Category, Quantity, Revenue
🔍 Key Insights
Peak sales occur during morning hours ☀️
Certain locations generate higher revenue & footfall
A few products contribute to the majority of sales
Average bill per person is around $4–5
